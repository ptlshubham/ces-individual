We are dedicated to safeguarding your personal information.

We just collect the bare minimum of information about you necessary to provide you with a quality service. This policy outlines the types of procedures that may lead to the collection of personal information about you. We have the right to gather that information based on your usage of our website.

1.Delivery of Online Courses:

All online courses will be made available to students immediately after payment has been processed. Students will be able to access the course materials through the institute's online learning platform.

2.Shipping of Physical Resources

If a student has ordered physical resources such as textbooks or study guides, the institute will ship the materials within 3-5 business days of receiving the order. The institute uses a reliable shipping carrier such as UPS or FedEx and will provide tracking information to the student via email.

3.Delivery Timeframe

The estimated delivery time for physical resources is 7-10 business days within the United States and 10-15 business days for international orders. The institute is not responsible for any customs or import duties that may be applied to international shipments.

4.Shipping Costs

The cost of shipping physical resources will be added to the total cost of the order and will be displayed during the checkout process. The institute offers free shipping for orders over $50 within the United States.

5.Returns and Refunds

If a student is not satisfied with a physical resource, they may return it within 30 days of receipt for a full refund. The institute will cover the cost of return shipping. In the case of online courses, no refunds will be given once the course has been accessed.

6.Lost or Damaged Orders:

The institute takes care to package all physical resources securely to prevent damage during shipping. However, if a student receives a damaged or lost order, they should contact the institute's customer service team immediately. The institute will either replace the damaged or lost resources or provide a full refund.
