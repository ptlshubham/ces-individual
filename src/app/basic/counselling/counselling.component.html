Introducing Online Counselling Sessions with Dr. Jigar Jani for Students

We are delighted to announce that students now have the opportunity to book online counselling sessions with Dr. Jigar Jani, a renowned professional in the field. These sessions can be conveniently conducted over Google Meet, providing a secure and accessible platform for students seeking guidance.

To schedule a session, you have two simple options. You can either submit your details in the form below, or directly contact us via WhatsApp by sharing your name, class, and institute information. Once connected, you will receive a Google Meet link that will enable you to participate in the online counselling sessions.

These sessions serve as an invaluable resource for students seeking support in various aspects of their lives, including career-related concerns and overall well-being. Dr. Jigar Jani brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise, ensuring that students receive the guidance they need to navigate their academic and personal journeys successfully.

We encourage students to take advantage of this opportunity to engage in meaningful conversations and seek guidance from an experienced professional. By addressing any questions or challenges they may have, these online counselling sessions offer a valuable space for personal growth and development.

To book your online counselling session or for any further inquiries, please fill out the form below or reach out to us via WhatsApp. We look forward to assisting you on your path towards a fulfilling and successfulÂ future.
