Performing Arts Activities.

Strongly believing in the holistic development of students, CES has a dedicated centre for activities related to Theatre, Music, Drawing, Designing, Dance and animation for students. Here experts from respective field groom students and member of community to hone their talent in such performing arts.

Opportunities in Sports .

Through projects like “Project Arjun”, CES strive to help students excel in sports and other physical activities too. The dedicated training centre for shooting sports, crickets etc. are among most popular in student community. Student of CES has proved their mettle by competing and winning at national and state level tournaments in various sports and games.

Organic and Food Festival – Chatkaro

The traditional Indian food has taken off hugely at global scale. Taking note of that, CES organizes a festival titled “Chatkaro” for traditional and organic food. It is very popular among the local and state community. The central government’s goal of promoting millet foods has also been taken care through such festivals.

Project Anandam for Mental Peace and Stress Free Life

Charotar Education Society understands that the fast pace of life with peer and societal pressure for excelling in studies may put unnecessary mental burden on students. Hence, in order to help students come out of their mental stress and to improve their interpersonal skills, Project Anandam is launched. Through this project students can discreetly meet a counsellor for discussing and alleviating the stress causing issues. Such projects also help in keeping students away from malpractices of tobacco consumption and substance abuse.

Senior Citizen Club

The most important ocean of experience and wisdom any society has are its senior citizens. They are not only a treasure but also light house of future generation. Valuing the contribution of senior citizens, CES has a dedicated club of senior member of our society where they can discuss, guide and share their experience with younger generation on facets of life.

Statue Cleaning

Understanding and valuing the importance of local, state and national their statues are various places are installed by various bodies. But with time the neglection of such monuments detach the younger generation from their heroes. Hence we organize the activities of cleaning statues, in Charotar region, of such dignitaries through volunteering by our students and esteemed members. This way we try to preserve the legacy of our stalwarts.

Spreading awareness for superstition removal (AndhshraddhaNivaran)

The superstition, Andhashraddha, is still prevalent in some members of society, especially in villages. CES invites esteemed member of society, scientists, doctors, NGO to demonstrate to public, through scientific demonstrations, the importance of ration and logical thinking in helping them give up their superstitions.

Left-over food distribution

We volunteer in collection of leftover foods from places where occasions are held including those at marriage functions, restaurants etc. The food is then distributed to needy people, and even to animal houses, in hygienic packets. For that we have dedicated van and a team of volunteer.

Initiative Fee concession to Widow Mother Child.

Charotar Education Society in committed to the education of every child hence provides concession in school and college fees to those students who lost their father.

Fee concession to Army men’s Child.

Concession in fees is also given to the children of guards of national security, Army men.

Staff Medical Insurance.

Catering to the needs of health of staff, CES provides the medical insurance facility to all of its staff members. The medical expense incurred on various diseases, operation and other ailments has proved boon for employees. We have helped many of our employees in their medical bills. The few examples are Kaushik Bhai, Bhojanalya Staff and a sweeper ben.

Tie-up with Red Cross Society.

“Every drop of blood counts”. Keeping this motto in mind, CES has forged an agreement with Red Cross Society. Through Red Cross Society, the blood donation camps are regularly. It has saved lives of thousands of people.

Tie-up with Shivani Foundation

Through a tie-up with Dr. Shivani Bhatt foundation, CES has helped many female students from underprivileged section of society in their education needs and helping them in their medical needs also.

Vaidarbhi Tapovan

This is an initiative wherein CES is working with Children University, Gandhinagar for integrated maternal and child services, including mental and spiritual health services.

Staff Health Check-up camps

Regular camps are organized for all CES family members in order to keep them fit, vigilant and healthy.

Cervical cancer Vaccination Camp

CES and various Donner’s is providing free Cervical Cancer Vaccine to female student of various schools it manages. Hundreds of such females can now be relieved that they won’t suffer from such deadly diseases.

Breast Cancer Checkup camp

Keeping the importance of early indication of any malfunction in body, Brest Cancer Check-up camps too are regularly organized.
