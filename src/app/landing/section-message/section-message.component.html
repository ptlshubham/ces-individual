Welcome to Charotar Education Society

Charotar Education Society, Anand, which was established on 16th April 1916 by the "Gem of Charotar" Shri Motibhai Amin Saheb with a view to developing higher education in the Charotar Pradesh, the region between the Mahi and Vatrak rivers extending from Vasad to Kheda. The Constitution of the Society was framed by Late Shri Vithalbhai Patel, the elder brother of Sardar Vallabhabhai Patel, who played an active role in the preparation of the Indian Constitution.
